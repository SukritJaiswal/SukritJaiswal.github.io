Outreach

Aside from my academic pursuits, I actively participate in extracurricular activities and community outreach programs. I achieved the title of ICSE National Level Quiz Champion in 2016 and thoroughly enjoyed engaging in various academic events. In 2020, I had the honor of being a dedicated member of the International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) team at IISER Pune. Our mission revolved around raising awareness about preventive measures and combatting drug resistance related to Malaria, a deadly mosquito-borne disease caused by the Plasmodium parasite. Our focus was particularly on rural areas in India. My unwavering motivation to give back to society and share scientific knowledge inspired me to engage with underprivileged students through the IISER Pune Social Awareness Initiative, known as DISHA. Furthermore, I'm an active member of the IISER Pune Quiz Club, Astronomy Club, Mimamsa Organisation Team, and the Incubation Centre and Career Development Cell. The diverse skills and experiences I've gained through these involvements have equipped me to contribute to the cultivation and promotion of a dynamic and thriving work environment.
